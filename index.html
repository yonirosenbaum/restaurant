About us

Our Story

Cafe Italia started out as our vision after seeing after visiting Southern Italy and seeing the beautiful culture.
Our dream was to bring together people in an authentic Italian environment with delicious food.
A place where friends could would meet, people could escape the office for a relaxing capacciuno and couples could share a bite and discuss they're day in a tranquil light-hearted atmosphere.
A place where you could feel at home and thousands of miles away.
We started our dreams with a small cafe style barely large enough to accommodate 2 families and quickly thereafter we outgrew that and the following building.
Energised by our dreams we bought out a family restuarant and renovated it to give you a real Italian experience.
It was perfect.
A dining area with carefully crafted wooden chairs.
A patio where you can feel the cool sea breeze.
A kitchen where we could be creative and expand our menu, and that we did.
